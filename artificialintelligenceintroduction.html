artificial intelligence introduction:

Artificial intelligence is one of the branches of computer science, and one of the basic elements on which the technology industry is based today. The term artificial intelligence - which is referred to in short (AI) - can be defined as the ability of digital machines and computers to perform certain tasks and applications are used in a wide range of fields such as: medical diagnostics, Internet search engines, natural language processing, video games, stock trading, law, image discrimination and analysis, toys, scientific discoveries, automatic control, and voice recognition
