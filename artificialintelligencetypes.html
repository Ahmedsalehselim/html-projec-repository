Types of artificial intelligence 1- Limited or narrow artificial intelligence: it is one of the types of artificial intelligence that can perform specific and clear tasks, such as self-driving cars, or even speech or image recognition programs. 2- General artificial intelligence: It is the type that can work with a capacity similar to the human ability in terms of thinking, as it focuses on making the machine capable of thinking and planning on its own and similar to human thinking 3 - Superior artificial intelligence: It is the type that may exceed the level of human intelligence, which can do tasks better than the specialized and knowledgeable person.
